Our Story

Why Blur Exists

Online dating taught us to judge people before knowing them.
Matrimony taught us to filter people before meeting them.
Somewhere in between, people became profiles.

Blur started with a simple question:

What if knowing came before seeing?

The Name

People are blurry when you first meet them.

Not because you can't see them. Because you don't know them yet.

The more you talk, the clearer they become.

The Logo

A silhouette before a face. A signal before an image. A person before a profile.

The logo is not decoration. It's part of the philosophy.

What We Believe

  • Attraction grows.
  • Privacy creates honesty.
  • Curiosity matters more than chemistry.
  • Good conversations deserve time.
  • People are more than profiles.
